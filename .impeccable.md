## Design Context

### Users
Solo founders, indie hackers, vibe coders, and AI builders evaluating startup ideas before committing to build. They are technically literate, time-pressured, and skeptical of hype. They use the product during active work sessions — late nights, weekend sprints, Sunday idea runs. The job: get a market signal in 5 minutes before wasting months.

### Brand Personality
**Bold. Direct. No-nonsense.**
Three words: *punchy, confident, irreverent*

The brand says "stop building the wrong thing" — not "we'd love to help you explore." It has opinions. It calls the work "founder diligence" and means it. The tone is the confident senior engineer who's shipped a few things: no hedging, no filler, no wishy-washy CTAs.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Neobrutalism** — not the ironic web3 kind, the industrial-strength kind.
- Primary: `#FFE000` yellow + `#111` near-black
- Borders: 2–2.5px solid `#111`, box-shadow offsets `2px 2px 0 #111` or `3px 3px 0 #111`
- Typography: Nunito 700/800/900 for brand elements; Roboto 400/700 for template chrome
- No gradients, no glassmorphism, no purple. The purple CSS variables in `globals.css` are legacy drift — do not use them.
- Theme: **light** (used during active work hours by builders, not consumed passively at night)
- The slot-machine reel is the product's signature UI — comic-book energy is native to the brand

**Retro comic / pop-art** is an approved visual direction for illustrations only. Halftone dots, bold outlines, flat colors (#FFE000, #FF3B3B, #111), starburst shapes, speech bubbles, action lines. This energy fits because the product itself is dramatic — "validated!" or "not worth building" are high-stakes outputs.

**Anti-references**: generic SaaS light-mode (Inter + blue CTA + white card grid), purple-to-blue gradients, glassmorphism, "AI startup" aesthetic.

### Design Principles
1. **Every word earns its place.** No filler copy. Cut before shipping.
2. **Yellow is the signal.** Use #FFE000 for exactly one thing per section — the most important action or number. Diluting it kills the signal.
3. **Borders over shadows.** Neobrutalist borders convey weight without blur. Prefer `box-shadow: 2px 2px 0 #111` over `drop-shadow`.
4. **Mobile is primary.** The typical user spins the wheel on their phone. Every touch target ≥ 44px. No text below 13px.
5. **Comic energy, founder credibility.** Playful visuals (illustration, starburst badges) must co-exist with trust signals (no subscription, secure checkout, credits never expire). Don't let one undermine the other.
